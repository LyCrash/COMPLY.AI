PRIVACY POLICY - ACME CORPORATION

Last Updated: January 2020

1. DATA COLLECTION

We collect all data from users when they visit our website. This includes but is not limited to:
- Full name, email, phone number, address
- IP addresses, browsing history, cookies
- Credit card information
- Social security numbers
- Medical records
- Location data (24/7 tracking)

By using our service, you automatically consent to all data collection.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We may use your personal information for any purpose we deem necessary, including:
- Marketing and advertising (we will share with our 500+ partners)
- Selling to third parties
- Background checks
- Training AI models
- Any future purposes we decide

3. DATA SHARING

Your data will be shared with:
- All our business partners
- Marketing agencies worldwide
- Government entities
- Anyone who requests it
- Third-party data brokers

We do not notify users when sharing their data.

4. DATA RETENTION

We keep your data forever. Even if you delete your account, we retain all information indefinitely for "business purposes."

5. YOUR RIGHTS

You have no right to:
- Access your data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data
- Object to processing
- Withdraw consent

6. CONTACT

If you have questions, too bad. We don't respond to privacy inquiries.

RGPD VIOLATIONS IN THIS POLICY:
- No explicit consent mechanism
- Pre-ticked consent by using service
- Unlimited data collection without justification
- No data minimization
- Sharing data without user knowledge
- Indefinite data retention
- No user rights (access, deletion, portability)
- No DPO contact information
- No legal basis for processing
- No information about international transfers
